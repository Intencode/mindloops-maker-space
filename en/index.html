About Program 


Get ready to unleash your creativity and technological prowess at the MindLoops® Maker Space competition! Happening in Johor, this electrifying event invites teams of four students to brainstorm, design, and develop cutting-edge STEM and TVET-based prototypes. Dive into exciting themes such as eco-friendly innovations, healthy living solutions, energy efficiency and others, and compete for the top spot.


Supported by esteemed partners like Jabatan Pendidikan Negeri Johor (JPNJ), Yayasan JCorp, Yayasan Raja Zarith Sofea, and Petrosains, this is your opportunity to turn your innovative ideas into reality.
