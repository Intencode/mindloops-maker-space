Frequently Asked Questions




Who can join the contest?  


All primary school children in Johor aged 10 to 12 are encouraged to participate. 


Is there any registration fee?  


No, it’s free to register the teams. 


How many students per team and how many teams can a school send?  


Each team consists of 4 students and 1 accompanying teacher. Schools can submit multiple teams, but not all teams will be selected to participate. 


When is the event and where is it located?  


The event is on 24 November 2024. Location is to be confirmed. 


Can I invite my family to attend the competition?  


Yes, family members can attend the event for free.
