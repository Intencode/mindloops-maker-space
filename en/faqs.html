Frequently Asked Questions




Who can join the contest?


All primary school children across Johor who are aged 10 to 12 years old are encouraged to participate in the event.


Is there any registration fee?


No, it’s free to register the teams.


How many students per team and how many teams can a school send?


Each team consists of 4 team members from one school. Each school may submit multiple teams to participate. However, not all teams will be selected to participate.


When is the event and the location?


The event date is 24 November 2024 at (location TBC).


Can I invite my family to attend the competition?


Yes, you may invite your family members to attend the event as the attendees (Free Admission).
