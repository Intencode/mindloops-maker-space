Main Activities


Are you Malaysia's next best bright kids?


MindLoops® Maker Space is looking for a team of 5 kids who have what it takes to develop solutions for our local businesses.


What is MindLoops® Maker Space?


MindLoops® Maker Space is a hands-on collaborative program that brings together like-minded students to co-develop a prototype by using concepts from STEM and technical skills from TVET.


How can I participate?


  1. Simply form a group of 4 from members of your school to participate. Remember to bring your teacher to support you!
  2. Work together with your team to develop the idea and concept to produce a prototype based on the themes selected.
  3. Submit your brilliant idea and concept for our juries to assess.
  4. Selected teams will be invited to join the main event.
  5. Unleash your creativity by developing the prototype on the event day.
  6. Present your wonderful prototype to our judges.
  7. Winners will be awarded prizes!
