MindLoops® Maker Space Guidelines


Welcome to MindLoops® Maker Space

Maker space is a hands-on collaborative learning experience for young innovators like your can explore, create, and learn.


Our commitment


  • Safety first: Your safety is our top priority. Always follow the safety guidelines provided by our friendly facilitators.
  • Respect and collaboration: Treat fellow makers with kindness and respect. Collaboration is key!
  • Curiosity and Creativity: Let your curiosity lead the way. Experiment, tinker, and dream big!
  • Clean up: Leave the space better than you found it. Put tools and materials back where they belong.

What You Can Do


  • Explore Tools and Materials: Get hands-on with various tools, from laser cutters to sewing machines.
  • Design Challenges: Take part in fun design challenges. Can you build a bridge using only popsicle sticks?
  • Learn New Skills: Discover coding, woodworking, and more. You’re capable of amazing things!
  • Create your own masterpieces: Design and build your own creations. Cardboard sculptures anyone?

What You Should Know


  • Age group: Maker Space is designed for kids aged 10 to 12 years old.
  • Supervision: Facilitators will accompany the participants during the activity.
  • Event Duration: 9 am to 5 pm
  • Membership: Consider joining our membership to receive exclusive benefits!

Have Fun!
We can’t wait to see what you’ll create at Maker Space! Let your imagination soar, and remember: You’re a maker, capable of turning ideas into reality.
