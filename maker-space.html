Main Activities


Are you Malaysia's next best bright kids?


MindLoops® Maker Space is looking for a team of 5 kids who have what it takes to develop solutions for our local businesses.


What is MindLoops® Maker Space?


MindLoops® Maker Space is a hands-on collaborative program that brings together like-minded students to co-develop a prototype by using concepts from STEM and technical skills from TVET.


How can I participate?


  1. Simply form a group of 5 from members of your school to participate. Remember to bring your teacher to support you!
  2. Work together with your team to develop the idea and concept to produce a prototype based on the themes selected.
  3. Submit your brilliant idea and concept for our juries to assess.
  4. Selected teams will be invited to join the main event.
  5. Unleash your creativity by developing the prototype on the event day.
  6. Present your wonderful prototype to our judges.
  7. Winners will be awarded prizes!

Themes


  • Eco-friendly innovations
  • Healthy living solutions
  • Sustainable gardening
  • Fashion and accessories
  • Wildlife and nature conversation
  • Energy efficiency
  • Recycling and upcycling
  • Water conservation

  • Community impact projects

Frequently Asked Questions


Who can join the contest?


All primary school children across Johor who are aged 10 to 12 years old are encouraged to participate in the event.


Is there any registration fee?


No, it’s free to register the teams.


How many students per team and how many teams can a school send?


Each team consists of 4 team members from one school. Each school may submit multiple teams to participate. However, not all teams will be selected to participate.


When is the event and the location?


The event date is 24 November 2024 at (location TBC).


How to register?


Interested teams are encouraged to submit their application to this form (open until 7 September 2024).


Can I invite my family to attend the competition?


Yes, you may invite your family members to attend the event as the attendees (Free Admission).
